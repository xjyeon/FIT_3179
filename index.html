Religion of 1026 Respondents

Using a SurveyMonkey poll, conducted between July 29 and August 1, 2016, we asked 1026 respondents their religion and questions about public displays of religion. The radial plot on the right displays the religion of 1026 respondents. It is clear that out of the respondents, 238 reponsdents didn't associate themselves with any religion, and 232 were protestants.
